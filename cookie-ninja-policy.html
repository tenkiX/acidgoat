Cookie Ninja - Privacy Policy


Effective Date: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

I respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the data of my players. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you play my game.

2. Information We Collect Automatically

While I do not use any first-party analytics tools to track your behavior, our third-party service providers may collect certain technical data to provide essential game services:

Unity LevelPlay (Ads): To serve advertisements, Unity may collect your IP address, device identifiers (like IDFA or Google Advertising ID), and information about how you interact with ads.

In-App Purchases (IAP): Transactions are handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not see or store your credit card information. We only receive a "receipt" from the store to confirm your purchase was successful.

Cloud Saves (GameKit & Play Services): To save your progress across devices, we use Apple GameKit (iOS) and Google Play Games Services (Android). These services use your platform-specific Account ID to store your game data (e.g., levels, resources, and siege progress).

3. How We Use Your Data

We and our partners use the collected data strictly for:

Game Functionality: Storing your progress and restoring purchases.

Advertising: Providing rewarded and interstitial ads via Unity LevelPlay.

Security & Fraud Prevention: Protecting against unauthorized transactions and bot activity.

4. Third-Party Links & SDKs

Our game integrates the following third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

Unity: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Google Play Services: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Apple Game Center: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
